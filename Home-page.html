Welcome to Know Animals

Animals is a big part of our eco-system. Animals provides us with food, livestock, and clothing for our daily use. All animals are important as they provide balance in the eco-system. Many people treat animals as companions as they offer love and affections that helps us humans to be able to relax and be happy with their presence.

Purpose

Know Animals helps the people to be informed by the different animals around the world. Helping the animals and maintaing the balance in the world is the goal of Know Animals.
